FAQ

Common Questions

What should I wear to my color analysis? +

Come with a clean face (no makeup) and wear a neutral top (white, gray, or black). Avoid jewelry near your face, and pull hair back if possible. This lets us see your natural coloring without interference.

Do you offer virtual color analysis? +

Yes! We offer virtual color analysis via Zoom. You'll need good natural lighting and a few items we'll discuss during booking. While in-person draping is ideal, our virtual sessions are highly accurate and convenient.

What is the 12-season color system? +

The 12-season system expands on the traditional 4 seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter) by adding three sub-types each: Light, True/Pure, and Deep/Dark. For example, you might be a Light Spring, True Spring, or Warm Spring. This gives a much more precise and personalized color palette.

How long until I receive my color palette? +

You'll leave your session knowing your season and best colors. Your complete digital color palette, lookbook, and shopping guide will be emailed within 3-5 business days after your appointment.

Can I bring a friend? +

Absolutely! We offer duo sessions at a discounted rate. It's a great girls' day activity. Contact us for duo pricing on any of our services.
